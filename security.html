Security is mostly hygiene and consistency.

Baseline checklist

  • Use a secret manager. No secrets in git.
  • Least privilege by default.
  • Patch dependencies. Automate updates where possible.
  • Log auth events and admin actions.
  • Backups + restore drills.

Don’t

  • Don’t roll your own crypto.
  • Don’t rely on “security through obscurity”.
