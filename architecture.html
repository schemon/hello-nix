Architecture is the set of constraints that keep you fast later.

Rules of thumb

  • Start monolithic. Split when you have pain + evidence.
  • Prefer clear boundaries (modules) before distributed systems.
  • Write ADRs (Architecture Decision Records) for big calls.
  • Make failure modes explicit: what happens when X is down?

Deployments

  • Automate deploys. Human clicks are a reliability bug.
  • Keep environments consistent. Drift kills.
